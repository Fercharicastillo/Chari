1 Introducción

Welcome to the website of Professor Chari FerNaNdo!

Here you will find a wide range of resources and support materials for learning mathematics and physics. As a math and physics teacher, I am pleased to share with you a variety of tools and content that will help you improve your understanding and mastery of these fundamental topics.

On this page, they will be able to access a wide range of resources, such as interactive simulators, university homework, science projects, relevant scientific articles, advanced graphers, and PDF files. These resources were carefully selected to provide you with relevant and practical support material in your learning process.

In addition, I am happy to provide you with instruction and guidance in various tools and programming languages that can be of great use in academic and professional settings. They will learn to use L aTeX for creating quality scientific papers and presentations, JavaScript for adding interactivity to your web projects, R for data analysis, CSS and HTML for web page design and development, MathLab for complex data processing and calculations, and Tikz for generating high-quality graphics.

I am committed to providing you with an enriching and accessible learning experience. I hope this website becomes your trusted resource, and that you find inspiration and knowledge through the materials and tools available here.

I encourage you to explore all that this page has to offer and to make the most of the resources at your disposal. Feel free to contact me if you have any questions, comments, or suggestions. Enjoy your learning and make the most of this valuable resource!
